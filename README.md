# mobileProject


Our app’s goal is to be a clicker game, like for example the most known one of the genre, Cookie Clicker. End users are basically everyone, starting from toddlers to old people in rest homes. Anyone can click on the screen and clicker games are pretty addicting in their own way if you start playing them.
So how does the game work? Well, you click on the *soon to be* job application paper and you get a “rejected job” point which you can use in the upgrade shop for more powerups and the last upgrade will be getting a job and thus “winning” the game. Winning in brackets since the game is unlimited, and you can still try to horde as much points as you want.
The game starts with a table, a paper and a lot of hopes for getting a job. The click animation will be a stamper which presses on the paper with a brand new red “Rejected“ stamp and the paper has an animation in which the paper swipes away from the table after getting rejected. We are trying to implement our own CVS also behind a chance system, so it would be a funny easter egg. 


Now. let me explain how the game will look like.

Firstly there is on the upper right, the rejection points, which you can use in the shop. How do you get to the shop you may ask, well there will be an arrow which says “swipe” and you will get to the upgrade shop. When in shop you can browse the catalog that has plenty of upgrades with different scaling prices. Lets return to the main screen. You can now see after buying the first upgrade that a coffee mug has appeared on to the table, well that’s just one of the visual upgrades you will be getting. If you swipe to the left, you can either A. Save your data onto our database or B. Fetch your data from our database and keep on playing afterwards. If you find yourself too invested in the game and you have beaten it once already you can also reset your data, and thus starting from scratch all over again.
